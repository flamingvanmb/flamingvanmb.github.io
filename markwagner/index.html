Mark Wagner - Architect

Mark Wagner - Architect is a residential architectural practice in Cambridge, Massachusetts. MWA specializes in the renovation of older New England homes, maintaining their historic integrity while providing everything that is needed for modern living.

Mark is an attentive listener, paying careful attention to his client's needs and wants. He provides intelligent and clear design and thoughtful construction supervision.

Mark, a registered architect since 1986, worked as a house framer before receiving his BFA from the School of the Museum of Fine Arts/Tufts and his B. Arch from the Boston Architectural Center.
