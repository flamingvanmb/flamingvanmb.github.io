Mark Wagner -- Architect

Mark Wagner – Architect is a residential architectural practice in Cambridge, Massachusetts.  MWA specializes in renovation for older New England homes, maintaining their historic integrity while providing everything that is needed for modern living. 

Mark is an attentive listener and attunes himself with the clients' needs and wants.  This is not ego driven architecture.  Rather this is a service that provides spatial organization, design and construction supervision so that houses become the homes his clients have dreamed of. 

Mark has worked as an architect for over thirty years.  He was educated at Tufts University, the School of the Museum of Fine Arts and the Boston Architectural Center.
